What are the role of values, empathy and self awareness in learning and programming?
Self-awareness is the ability to see yourself clearly and objectively through reflection and introspection. It allows us to see things from the perspective of others, practice self-control, work creatively and productively, and experience pride in ourselves and our work as well as general self-esteem. Self-awareness can make us better at our jobs, better communicators in the workplace, and enhance our self-confidence and job-related wellbeing.
If we are talking about Empathy, it allows us to build social connections with other people. Empathy can help you better understand other people, their feelings and whatâ€™s driving their behavior if you look at them without any judgment. Also empathy can help us to learn how to regulate our own emotions. It allows you to manage what you are feeling in the difficult situation or even in times of great stress without becoming overwhelmed.
So, as you can see Self-awareness and Empathy are very important and powerful tools that you can use in your learning process and promote high commitment and cooperation in the workplace.
